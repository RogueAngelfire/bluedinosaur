About Blue Dinosaur Animation

Blue Dinosaur Animation is the name under which artist Peter Collins creates stop motion animated productions. From the beginning with concept drawings, storyboards, puppet and set designs. Then the construction of said sets and puppets to the slow process of the animation taking one frame at a time.

Inspired by the legendary Ray Harryhausen and the work of many artists that have followed him in working in this medium. The name Blue Dinosaur is after a Harryhausen character "Gwangi" from the film "The Valley of Gwangi".

Most commissions have currently been music videos and advertising on social media.
